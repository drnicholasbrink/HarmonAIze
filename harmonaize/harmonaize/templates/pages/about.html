HarmonAIze

An open-source toolkit that streamlines climate and health data harmonisation, leveraging AI-assisted workflows for accelerated research insights.

Our Mission

We're building HarmonAIze to democratise climate-health research by providing researchers worldwide with powerful, accessible tools for data harmonisation. Our goal is to accelerate scientific discovery and enable evidence-based insights that can inform policy and improve global health outcomes.

Core Modules

Three specialised modules work together to provide comprehensive data harmonisation capabilities

{% include 'components/svg_icon.html' with icon='hospital' size='32' color='white' %}

Health Module

Specialized tools for harmonising health variables across diverse datasets. Standardizes medical terminology, units, and coding systems while maintaining data integrity for cross-study comparisons.

{% include 'components/svg_icon.html' with icon='map-pin' size='32' color='white' %}

Geolocation Module

Secure geolocation services for health facilities and addresses. Enables spatial analysis while protecting sensitive location data through privacy-preserving federated approaches to geocoding.

{% include 'components/svg_icon.html' with icon='thermometer' size='32' color='white' %}

Climate Module

Secure linkage and integration of climate data with health records. Provides access to curated environmental datasets while ensuring privacy-preserving connections between climate and health variables.
