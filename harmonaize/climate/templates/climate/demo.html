What Happens Behind the Scenes

Spatial Matching

Our system automatically finds the nearest climate data points to your study locations, handling coordinate transformations and spatial interpolation as needed.

Temporal Alignment

Climate data is matched to your study timeframes, aggregating daily, monthly, or seasonal data based on your research requirements.

Quality Assurance

Automatic quality checks ensure data integrity, handling missing values and flagging potential outliers in the integrated dataset.
