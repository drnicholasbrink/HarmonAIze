Best Practices for Health Research
Recommended Combinations:
  • Vector-borne Disease Studies: Temperature, precipitation, NDVI, humidity
  • Respiratory Health: Air quality (NO₂, AOD), temperature, humidity
  • Heat-related Illness: Land surface temperature, air temperature, urban heat islands
  • Water-borne Disease: Precipitation, surface water, flooding indicators
  • Social Determinants: Population density, nighttime lights, built-up areas
Spatial Considerations

Different datasets have varying spatial resolutions. The climate module can aggregate or buffer data around your study locations to match your research needs.
