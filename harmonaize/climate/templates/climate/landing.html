How Climate Integration Works

A simple four-step process to enrich your health data with environmental context

1

Select Your Study

Choose a study that has location and date information. Our system will identify the spatial and temporal scope for climate data retrieval.

2

Configure Variables

Select relevant climate variables from our curated collection. Choose from temperature, precipitation, air quality, and many more environmental indicators.

3

Automated Retrieval

Our system automatically fetches climate data for your study locations and timeframes, handling spatial interpolation and temporal alignment.

4

Export & Analyse

Download your enriched dataset with seamlessly integrated climate variables. Ready for statistical analysis and research insights.
